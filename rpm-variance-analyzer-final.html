Method note — Required RPM is derived as: Engine Warranted Speed = Warranted Speed ÷ (1 − Slip), then Required RPM = Engine Warranted Speed × 1,852 ÷ (Pitch × 60). Variance = (Required RPM − Reported RPM) ÷ Required RPM — positive means the vessel ran below the RPM needed for the warranted speed at that day's slip. The RPM method was rejected as a standalone basis for proving underperformance in The Divinegate; treat results here as one analytical input alongside independently verified weather and slip benchmarks, not a self-sufficient claim.
